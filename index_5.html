About XPGains

XPGains is a gamified gym tracker that rewards your consistency. Track your workouts and watch your progress as 14 muscle groups level up from 1 to 99 based on your training volume.

Every set you log earns XP calculated from weight lifted, reps performed, and exercise type. The more you train, the higher your levels climb—giving you a clear, motivating way to see your fitness journey unfold.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is XP calculated?

XP is calculated based on the weight you lift, the number of reps you perform, and the type of exercise. Compound exercises award more base XP than isolation exercises. Additional bonuses apply for training neglected muscles, maintaining consistency, and varying your routine.

What is the maximum level?

Each of the 14 muscle skills can be leveled from 1 to 99, giving a maximum Total Level of 1,386. The XP curve is designed for long-term progression—reaching level 99 in a single skill takes years of dedicated training.

Is my data saved if I delete the app?

Your workout data is stored locally on your device. If you use the Share Stats feature, your stats are also saved to our cloud database. We recommend sharing your stats regularly to maintain a backup.

Can I use XPGains offline?

Yes! All core features work offline. Your workouts are saved locally on your device. The Friends feature requires an internet connection to sync with other users.

How do I change the language or theme?

Go to the Settings tab to change your language (6 available) or switch between Classic and Mithril themes.

What is Spillover XP?

Compound exercises work multiple muscle groups. XPGains automatically awards a percentage of your XP to secondary muscles. For example, Bench Press gives bonus XP to Triceps and Delts in addition to your Chest XP.

Can I import my existing workout data?

Yes! Use the Calibrate Skills feature in Settings to manually set your skill levels if you're switching from another fitness tracker or want to account for your training history.
