# Privacy Policy for XPGains

**Last Updated: January 31, 2026**

## Overview

XPGains ("the App") is a fitness tracking application developed by Gerbert Guliyev. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device:
- Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
- XP and level progress for each muscle group
- Custom exercises and training plans
- App preferences (language, theme, units)
- Equipment settings

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server unless you choose to use the optional sharing features.

### Data Shared Optionally (Friends Feature)

If you choose to share your stats with friends, the following information is uploaded to our secure cloud database (Supabase):
- Display name (chosen by you)
- Muscle group levels and XP totals
- Total level

This data is shared voluntarily and can be deleted at any time by removing your shared profile.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Local data**: Used solely to provide app functionality and track your fitness progress
- **Shared data**: Used only to display your stats to friends you connect with

## Data Security

- Local data is stored using standard iOS secure storage
- Cloud data is transmitted via encrypted HTTPS connections
- We use Supabase, a trusted database provider with industry-standard security

## Third-Party Services

The App uses Supabase for optional cloud storage. Supabase's privacy policy can be found at: https://supabase.com/privacy

## Data Retention

- Local data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data
- Shared cloud data can be deleted at any time through the app's settings

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is designed for general fitness tracking and contains no inappropriate content.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (all data is visible within the app)
- Delete your data (clear local data in settings, remove shared profile)
- Choose not to share any data (the app works fully offline)

## No Advertising or Data Sales

- The App contains no advertisements
- We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties
- We do not use your data for marketing purposes

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date.
